## Startup Costs Breakdown

### Minimal Launch ($50-150)
- Etsy listing fees: $0.20 per listing (budget 40 listings = $8)
- Basic photography setup: smartphone + white poster board + natural light ($0)
- Shipping supplies starter kit: $25-50
- First month advertising test budget: $25-50
- Payment processing: 6.5% per transaction (no upfront cost)

### Competitive Launch ($300-800)
- Listing fees for 100+ items: $20+
- Lightbox or photography kit: $40-80
- Branding materials (stickers, tissue paper, thank-you cards): $50-100
- Shipping scale: $25-40
- Label printer: $80-150
- Three-month advertising budget: $150-300
- Professional logo design: $50-100

### Timeline to First Sale
- Shop setup and first listings: Week 1-2
- SEO indexing and visibility: Week 2-6
- First organic sale (typical): Week 3-8
- Consistent daily sales: Month 3-6
- Full-time income potential: Month 8-18

## Shop Setup Fundamentals

### Choosing Your Shop Name
- Keep it memorable and 4-20 characters
- Avoid trademarked terms or brand names
- Check availability across social media platforms before committing
- Consider searchability: does the name hint at what you sell?
- Avoid numbers, hyphens, or hard-to-spell words

### Shop Policies That Build Trust
- Clearly state processing times (be conservative, then over-deliver)
- Define your return and exchange policy explicitly
- Explain shipping methods and estimated delivery windows
- Address custom orders: do you accept them? What is the process?
- Include care instructions for your product category

### Shop Sections Strategy
- Create 5-10 logical sections that mirror how customers browse
- Use keyword-rich section names (not cute internal names)
- Ensure every listing belongs to a section
- Reorder sections to put best sellers first

## Listings Optimization

### Title Formula
Structure: Primary Keyword + Secondary Keyword + Descriptors + Occasion/Use

Example flow:
- Weak: "Pretty Blue Earrings"
- Strong: "Sapphire Blue Stud Earrings, Sterling Silver Gemstone Posts, September Birthstone, Minimalist Everyday Jewelry"

Rules:
- Use all 140 characters
- Front-load the most important search terms
- Include material, color, size, occasion, and style
- Never repeat the same word within a title
- Skip punctuation that wastes characters (use commas to separate phrases)

### Tags Strategy (13 Tags Per Listing)
- Use all 13 tags on every listing without exception
- Each tag can be up to 20 characters
- Use multi-word phrases, not single words
- Match tags to actual search queries customers use
- Include a mix of broad and specific terms
- Rotate seasonal tags (gift for mom, Valentine gift, Christmas present)
- Never duplicate words already in your title in single-word tags

### Description Framework
Paragraph 1: Hook and primary benefit (what problem does this solve or what joy does it bring?)
Paragraph 2: Detailed specifications (dimensions, materials, weight, colors)
Paragraph 3: How it is made or what makes it special
Paragraph 4: Shipping and handling details
Paragraph 5: Care instructions
Paragraph 6: Call to action (favorite the shop, check other listings)

### Category and Attributes
- Select the most specific subcategory available
- Fill in every attribute field Etsy provides
- Attributes directly affect search filtering, so missing attributes mean missed customers
- Update attributes when Etsy adds new options

## Product Photography

### Essential Shots Per Listing (Aim for 8-10 Photos)
1. Hero shot: clean, well-lit, product-focused on white or neutral background
2. Scale shot: product next to a common object or being held/worn
3. Detail shot: close-up of texture, stitching, material quality
4. Lifestyle shot: product in use or in a styled setting
5. Variation shot: show available colors, sizes, or options
6. Packaging shot: how it arrives (builds gift-giving confidence)
7. Process shot: behind-the-scenes of creation (builds authenticity)
8. Infographic shot: dimensions, features labeled on image

### Photography on a Budget
- Natural light near a large window (north-facing is ideal for consistent light)
- White foam board as a reflector to fill shadows
- Smartphone with portrait mode for shallow depth of field
- Free editing apps for white balance correction and brightness
- Consistent style across all listings builds brand recognition
- Shoot in batches to maintain consistency

### Photo Optimization
- First photo is the thumbnail: make it instantly clear what the product is
- Use the full frame; avoid tiny products with excessive white space
- Ensure colors are accurate to reduce returns
- Add text overlays sparingly and only for critical information (size, quantity)

## SEO and Search Strategy

### How Etsy Search Works
- Etsy matches query words to titles, tags, categories, and attributes
- Relevancy score is weighted by exact phrase matches
- Listing quality score improves with clicks, favorites, and purchases
- Recency gives a temporary boost to new and renewed listings
- Shop quality score factors in reviews, completion rate, and response time

### Keyword Research Process
1. Type partial phrases into Etsy search bar and note autocomplete suggestions
2. Browse competitor shops with high sales and study their titles and tags
3. Use the Etsy search analytics in your shop dashboard
4. Check related searches at the bottom of Etsy search results pages
5. Track which keywords drive your traffic in Stats section

### Long-Tail Keyword Strategy
- Broad terms have massive competition (example: "necklace" = millions of results)
- Long-tail terms convert better (example: "personalized name necklace gold filled")
- Target phrases with 3-5 words that describe exactly what you sell
- Create listings that target different long-tail variations of similar products

## Pricing Strategy

### Cost-Based Pricing Formula
Minimum Price = (Materials + Labor + Overhead + Etsy Fees) x 2

Breaking it down:
- Materials: every component including packaging
- Labor: pay yourself at least minimum wage (track your actual time)
- Overhead: photography, tools, workspace, software subscriptions
- Etsy fees: listing fee ($0.20) + transaction fee (6.5%) + payment processing (3% + $0.25)
- The 2x multiplier provides margin for promotions and wholesale

### Competitive Pricing Analysis
- Search your primary keywords and note the price range
- Identify where your quality and uniqueness positions you
- Do not race to the bottom; compete on value and presentation
- Premium pricing works when photos and descriptions justify it
- Test different price points and measure conversion rates

### Free Shipping Strategy
- Etsy gives search priority to listings with free shipping
- Build shipping cost into the item price
- Offer free shipping on orders over a threshold to increase average order value
- Be transparent: customers understand shipping is factored into pricing

## Shipping Operations

### Packaging Essentials
- Product protection is the priority; branding is secondary
- Test your packaging by shipping to yourself first
- Use appropriate box or mailer sizes to avoid dimensional weight surcharges
- Include a packing slip and a thank-you note
- Consider eco-friendly packaging as a selling point

### Shipping Cost Management
- Compare carrier rates for your typical package dimensions and weight
- Purchase shipping labels through Etsy for discounted rates
- Offer multiple shipping speeds when feasible
- Set accurate processing times and meet them consistently
- Communicate tracking information promptly

### International Shipping
- Start with domestic shipping until you have your process dialed in
- Research customs forms and prohibited items for target countries
- Price international shipping carefully to avoid losses
- Consider whether the margin justifies the complexity

## Scaling Strategies

### Month 1-3: Foundation
- Launch with 30-50 well-optimized listings
- Post at least 3-5 new listings per week
- Respond to all messages within 24 hours
- Collect reviews by providing exceptional experiences
- Study your shop stats weekly

### Month 3-6: Optimization
- Double down on your best-selling categories
- Retire listings with zero views after 60 days
- Introduce product bundles and sets
- Build an email list through package inserts
- Test Etsy Ads with a small daily budget on proven listings

### Month 6-12: Expansion
- Expand product lines based on customer feedback and requests
- Consider hiring help for production or shipping
- Explore wholesale and consignment opportunities
- Cross-promote on social media with content that provides value
- Develop repeat customer incentives

### Month 12+: Systemization
- Document all processes for delegation
- Build inventory management systems
- Diversify beyond Etsy (own website, other marketplaces)
- Invest in professional photography and branding
- Consider transitioning best sellers to production methods that scale

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Launching with fewer than 20 listings (insufficient to test the market)
- Copying competitor titles and descriptions word for word
- Using stock photos instead of original product photography
- Ignoring shop stats and not iterating on what works
- Underpricing to compete (this attracts price-sensitive customers and erodes margins)
- Not accounting for all fees in pricing
- Inconsistent posting schedule (the algorithm rewards regular activity)
- Taking negative reviews personally instead of using them to improve
- Trying to sell everything to everyone instead of finding a niche
- Neglecting customer communication and response times

## Metrics That Matter

Track these weekly:
- Views and visits (is your SEO working?)
- Conversion rate (are listings compelling?)
- Average order value (is your pricing and bundling effective?)
- Revenue per listing (which products earn their shelf space?)
- Repeat customer rate (are you building loyalty?)
- Search ranking for target keywords (is your position improving?)

## When to Seek Additional Help

- Revenue exceeds $1,000/month: consider bookkeeping software
- Spending more than 4 hours/day: evaluate what to outsource first
- Sales tax complexity: consult a tax professional
- Trademark or intellectual property questions: consult a legal professional
- Scaling production: research manufacturers or production partners
